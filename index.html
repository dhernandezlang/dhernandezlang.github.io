About

I am a PhD in astrophysics with a main interest in galaxy cluster cosmology, galaxy evolution, and Euclid photometry.

Currently working as a postdoctoral researcher for the Euclid collaboration at the Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München, München. My work is mainly focused on validating Euclid data and photometry, with a side interest in cosmology and galaxy evolution in extreme environments. When not doing science, I paint and do handicrafts.

2023
Planck DES Galaxy clusters

The PSZ-MCMF catalogue of Planck clusters over the DES region

I extended the number of galaxy clusters confirmed with Planck by going down to S/N of 3. For this, my collaborators created a catalog of cluster candidates within the DES area and I then modified/run the MCMF cluster confirmation algorithm to find the best optical counterparts of each candidate. The results yielded a cluster sample with 90% purity and 3 times as many clusters as previously reported.

2022
Galaxy evolution Cluster mergers

Clash of Titans: A MUSE dynamical study of the extreme cluster merger SPT-CL J0307-6225

In this work, I focus on the analysis of the merging galaxy cluster SPT-CL J0307-6225. Through an analysis of its merging dynamics, I separated the substructures and find a likely mass ratio of ~1.3. On the other hand, the analysis of the galaxy population suggests a previous merger within one of the substructures.
